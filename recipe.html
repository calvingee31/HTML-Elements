Double Cheeseburger Omelette

Ingredients

  • 2 Jumbo eggs
  • 1/3 of ground beef
  • One stick of butter
  • Ketcup
  • Store bought Shredded Cheddar Cheese

Procedures

Crack two eggs in the bowl then put a little bit of salt and pepper and then whisk it well. If you want something tasty for your egg, my choice is just to add a little bit of garlic powder or onion powder.

Steps

  1. Let start our beef filling, first put a small pot onto a stove and turn on medium heat.
  2. Once the small pot was heated, add the 1/3 ground beef into a small pot without oil. Because the beef creates oil.
  3. Start grinding the beef with your spatula and you don't need salt and pepper because ketchup creates that flavor.
  4. Once the beef was cooked, turn off the heat and start adding ketchup and stir it regularly.
  5. Now beef filling is done, it's time to create an omelette, first of all, I like to get a small frying pan because it's good for the flipping an omelette but if you using a regular size pan then it's hard to flip it.
  6. Now I will start put a small frying pan onto a stove and turn on medium heat
  7. First, grab your butter knife or regular knife then slice your stick of butter and put it on a small pan
  8. Once the butter was melted, add your half of your egg mixture and for bottom of the egg to cook for 5-6 minutes
