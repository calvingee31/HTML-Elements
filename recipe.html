Double Cheeseburger Omelette

Ingredients

  • 2 Jumbo eggs or 3-4 Medium eggs
  • 1/3 of ground beef
  • One stick of butter
  • Ketcup
  • Shreddered Cheddar Cheese

Procedures

Crack two eggs in the bowl then put a little bit of salt and pepper and then whisk it well. If you want something tasty for your egg, my choice is just to add a little bit of garlic powder or onion powder.
